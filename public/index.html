Welcome to the NCR stock exchange!

Here you can simulate purchasing various stocks. By default, an order can be placed even if the order is greater than the available balance in the funding account. To demonstrate what is possible with the Open APIs, you can link a First Digital demo account through the "Account" tab. Once an account is linked, the NCR stock exchange can look your balance before completing the order and can warn you if you do not have enough funds in your account.
